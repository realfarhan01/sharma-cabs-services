Sharma Cabs Services

Welcome to Sharma Cabs, your trusted travel partner in the heart of Uttarakhand! Beyond cab services, we also assist with hotel bookings and customized tour itineraries, providing end-to-end travel solutions.

At Sharma Cabs, we are committed to delivering exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for our customers. Let us handle the logistics while you focus on enjoying the stunning landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality of Uttarakhand. DLS Divine, Nainital

Located just a short walk from the iconic Naini Lake, DLS Divine is a popular choice for budget travelers. The hotel offers clean, comfortable rooms with essential amenities, making it a perfect base for exploring Nainital’s scenic beauty. Its central location ensures easy access to local markets, boat rides, and popular attractions like Mall Road and Tiffin Top. The friendly staff and warm hospitality add to the homely vibe, making your stay even more enjoyable. Whether you're traveling with family or friends, DLS Divine provides a cozy and affordable retreat amidst the serene hills of Nainital. Don’t miss the chance to savor delicious local cuisine at the in-house restaurant, which offers a variety of North Indian and Kumaoni dishes. With its unbeatable location and value-for-money services, DLS Divine is a top pick for travelers seeking comfort and convenience on a budget.

Lemon Tree, Rishikesh

Situated near the famous Laxman Jhula, Lemon Tree is a budget-friendly option for travelers seeking a peaceful stay in Rishikesh. The hotel provides simple yet well-maintained rooms, friendly service, and a convenient location close to the Ganges River, temples, and cafes. It’s an excellent choice for both spiritual seekers and adventure enthusiasts. The serene ambiance of the hotel allows guests to relax and rejuvenate after a day of exploring Rishikesh’s vibrant culture and natural beauty. From yoga and meditation to river rafting and trekking, Lemon Tree serves as a perfect base for all kinds of activities. The staff is always ready to assist with travel tips and local recommendations, ensuring a memorable stay. Additionally, the hotel’s proximity to popular attractions like Ram Jhula, Triveni Ghat, and the Beatles Ashram makes it a convenient choice for sightseeing. Whether you're here for spiritual solace or thrilling adventures, Lemon Tree offers a comfortable and affordable stay in the heart of Rishikesh.

Hotel Green Castle, Mussoorie

Nestled amidst the serene hills of Mussoorie, Hotel Green Castle offers breathtaking views of the Doon Valley. This budget hotel is known for its clean rooms, warm hospitality, and tranquil ambiance. It’s an ideal spot for travelers looking to unwind and enjoy the natural beauty of Mussoorie without spending a fortune. The hotel’s peaceful surroundings make it a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, allowing guests to reconnect with nature. Whether you're sipping a cup of tea on the balcony or taking a leisurely stroll in the nearby woods, the calm atmosphere is sure to rejuvenate your senses. The staff is attentive and always ready to assist with local sightseeing tips, ensuring you make the most of your stay. Popular attractions like Gun Hill, Camel’s Back Road, and Kempty Falls are just a short drive away, making it a convenient base for exploring Mussoorie. With its combination of affordability, comfort, and stunning views, Hotel Green Castle is a top choice for budget travelers seeking a memorable hill station experience.
